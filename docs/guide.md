# Guide: Planning around the long weekends

Watch the Sunday holidays — in 2026 that's Vesak Day (observed Mon 1 Jun), National Day (Mon 10 Aug) and Deepavali (Mon 9 Nov). Religious holidays such as Hari Raya and Deepavali are confirmed annually, so treat any forward calendar as a planning guide and confirm with MOM closer to the date.
